# Hacker as a Company – Morocco
## Charter

### Purpose
- Give Moroccan hackers a legal and ethical way to work.
- Help companies and institutions access trusted, local talent.
- Connect curiosity, skill, and responsibility under one name.

### Principles
- Freedom with discipline. Work legally and transparently.
- Respect scope, consent, and privacy at all times.
- No illegal activity under this name.
- Collaboration over competition.
- Share knowledge, credit fairly, and keep attribution (CC BY-SA 4.0).

### How We Work
- Members act as independent professionals or small teams.
- Missions include VDP support, bug bounty, training, and pentesting.
- All operations follow Moroccan law and DGSSI recommendations.
- Reports and deliverables must be clear, actionable, and client-ready.

### Joining
- Read and accept this Charter and the Code of Conduct.
- Open a “Join Request” issue in this repository.
- Leads may verify identity before client projects.

### Governance
- HAaC Morocco is a regional cell of the global movement.
- Decisions follow the Charter and collective agreement.
- The founder acts as steward, not owner.
